Political Science Honors Thesis: Constituency Size and Legislator Extremism in Lower House State Legislatures

In the Political Science Honors Program, I used multivariate regression analysis in order to examine the potential relationship between constituency size (number of voters in a district) and the political extremism of the legislators those districts produce. My results indicated a strong, positive relationship between a district’s size and a legislator’s extremism score
